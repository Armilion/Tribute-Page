The Undertaker

A legendary professional wrestler

Who has never heard of The Undertaker? Despite the cheesy reputation of pro wrestling, this name rings a bell for (almost? maybe?) everyone. The wrestling legend that is also known as The Deadman, The Phenom, The Demon of Death Valley or The American bad-ass, is a character that has been interpreted by Mark Calaway in the WWE. Earlier this year, he announced his retirement, after a 36 years (!) illustrious career and almost 30 loyal to the WWE. Now is a good time to pay tribute to man that may not have saved millions of lives (I'm looking at you Norman Borlaug), but has entertained at least as much. Without further ado, here are the top achievements of The Undertaker' career.

Highlights of The Undertaker's creer

1984 : before The Undertaker, the beginning of Mark Calaway's career

Mark Calaway makes his in-ring debut as a character named "Texas Red", at the World Class Championship Wrestling, a Texas federation. He will perform and lost his first match against Bruiser Brody.

Survivor Series, 1990 : The birth of the Undertaker persona

Mark Calaway makes is first ever WWE appearance at the Survivor Series pay per view in 1990. As the funeral march resonates within the arena, he shows up as a grim and sinister entity, who doesn't know fear and has death as his ally : The Undertaker. He fights in a 4v4 elimination tag team match and ends up in the winning team. A legend is born.

Wrestlemania VII, 1991 : The beginning of the Streak

One of the most famous, if not THE most famous accomplishment of The Undertaker, is his Wrestlemania's winning streak. It started at Wrestlemania VII against Jimmy "Superfly" Snuka. The legendary streak is on.

Survivor Series, 1991 : Undertaker's first WWE World title

On that day, Undertaker has to face one of the most pro wrestler that has ever stepped a foot in the ring : Hulk Hogan. For the first time in his career, Undertaker reached for the top of the compagny. His first solo world title out of seven.

Survivor Series, 1992 : First ever casket match

Undertaker faces off Kamala in a match specifialy designed to fit his gimmick : the casket match. The specificity? Put your opponent inside the casket, close it, win. Simple isn't it?

Badd blood, 1997 : First ever Hell in a Cell

Undertaker faces Shawn Michael in the first ever Hell in a Cell Match at the Badd Blood pay per view. In this gruesome format, wrestlers a locked down inside a 6 feet tall cage, with no disqualification, no count outs, anything goes. The only way to win is by pinfall or submission. The Deadman ends up losing the match due to his demon brother's intervention : Kane.

The Unforgiven, 1998 : First ever Inferno match

An other first time in history match... Following Kane's appeareance at Badd Blood, Undertaker and Kane started a long 2 years feud. As the rivalry unfolds, both protagonists face off in Kane's gimmick match : the Inferno match. For the first time ever, two wrestlers will face off in a ring surrounded by... fire. The winner is declared after one wrestler burns the other..

Judgment Day, 2000 : Undertaker becomes the American bad-ass

After an eight months break due to an injury in 1999, Undertaker comes back with a pretty different look.. Say goodbye to the grim graveyard tenant, welcome the typical bad-ass (some might say redneck) biker. The Undertaker is back and is here to kick some asses baby.

Survivor Series, 2003 : The Undertaker gets buried alive by his boss, Vince McMahon

After losing a WWE title opportunity due to the intervention of Vince McMahon in the previous pay per view, The Undertaker seeks revenge and challenges his boss to a Buried Alive Match. Unfortunately for him, his brother Kane came to help Vince, and buried him with a bulldozer.

Wrestlemania XX, 2004 : Dead man walking

Five months after losing to Vince McMahon, The Undertaker comes back at Wrestlemania, after weeks of enigmatic vignettes appearing on the weekly shows. However, he isn't a bad ass biker anymore... He resurrected from the dead and is now the grim and sinister entity he was before. The Undertaker means business and wants revenge from his brother Kane. However, the latter has no chance in Undertaker's graveyard : Wrestlemania. The streak continues, 12-0...

1998-2019 : The Brothers of Destruction

Despite their numerous rivalries over the years, Undertaker and Kane had joined forces at various occasions in the last two decades, wreaking havoc on their path. They even manage to win twice the WWE World Tag Team Championships.

Royal Rumble, 2007 : The Undertaker is going to Wrestlemania

At the Royal Rumble 2007, The Undertaker manages to win for the first time in his career the 30 man battle royal that takes place in the event, which gives him a title shot of his choice at Wrestlemania. He will go on to face Batista in one of the most epic rivalry of the decade.

Wrestlemania 25, 2009 : The greatest match in (Wrestlemania) history?

At Wrestlemania 25, The Undertaker faces a longtime rival : Shawn Michaels. The man that has never lost at Wrestlemania against the man that never lost to The Undertaker. A clash of style, a man that found God coming from heaven, against the demon coming from the depths of hell. The context is set, the alchemy between the two men is great, what comes of this is one of the most memorable wrestling matches of all time.

Wrestlemania XXX, 2014 : The Streak is over

As he grows older, Undertaker's apparition are more and more scarce. During the last decade, he almost exclusively appeared at Wrestlemania to defend his streak. However at Wrestlemania XXX, against Brock Lesnar, the unthinkable happen : in front of an incredulous crowd, Brock Lesnar manages to pin the Undertaker for the count of three. The Streak is over.

Hell in a Cell, 2015 : Undertaker gets revenge

One and a half year after losing The Streak to Brock Lesnar, The Undertaker challenges him to a revenge match at Summerslam. In a brutal encounter, The Phenom manages to win by submission.

Undertaker continued appearing once or twice a year for the five following years. During a documentary recollecting his last appearances in WWE, "The Last Ride", Undertaker announced his retirement in june 2020. For more informations about The Phenom, check his Wikipedia page
